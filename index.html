Music



Acoustic & folk/black metal

Nattsmyg is a music project formed in 2005 by Dan Heikenberg. The music ranges from acoustic to folk/black metal, and the lyrics are often about nature and swedish folklore. Several musicians have previously contributed to the albums and demos: Linn Carlshaf, Adam Nilsson, Robin Fors, Jacob Bramsell and Anton Hinterlach.

Metal music

Colloblast is a metal music project formed by Dan Heikenberg in 2020, with lyrics about space, the climate, and evolution. It exists both as a metal band with Dan Heikenberg (vocals/guitar), Anton Ryberg (Vocals/Bass) and Ludwig Havasi (Drums), and as a metal/drum&bass project with only Dan.

Games




Strategic Card Game (physical)



Stormshade is an advanced turn-based strategic card game for up to 2 players/deck, made by Dan Heikenberg, with artwork by Michelle Helen Tolo and Johan Aronson. Find areas and paths, build structures, hire workers and warriors, and use tactics and weather to your advantage.

One deck is enough for two players to play against each other, by sharing a deck. It is also possible to play with one deck each. That means you can play Asheim vs Vanheim if you have both these decks.

This game will be printed at a later time, or sooner if there is a growing demand.

Android/Steam Game

Cosmic Companions is a life simulator, made by Dan Heikenberg in 2022, in which you are an astronaut, travelling the universe to explore new worlds, and make first contact with aliens.

Explore, research, build and invite your new cosmic companions to your growing space community.

This game is currently in early production, and there is no release date yet.

Android/Steam Game

Mushroom Musical is a platformer game, made by Dan Heikenberg in 2021-2022, in which you are a Mushroom in search of its mushroom friends, who have dissapeared.

Jump to the pace of songs in different genres, through forests, fields, oceans and factories. Unlock challenge levels, new skins, and prepare yourself for a boss battle!
